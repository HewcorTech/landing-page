Network Infrastructure

A reliable network is the backbone of any modern home or business. We install and maintain whole-home low-voltage systems, including CAT6 and coax wiring, in-wall or surface data outlets, and all the essential hardware like switches, patch panels, firewalls, routers, and wireless access points. Whether it’s new construction, a renovation, or an upgrade, we trace, activate, and test every connection to make sure it works fast and reliably. From video calls while working from home to streaming and gaming, we make sure your network can handle everything your modern lifestyle demands.

Security and Access control

Keeping your home or small business safe doesn’t have to be complicated. We design and install comprehensive security systems powered by Ring Alarm, including cameras with motion detection and live streaming, control panels, door and window sensors, smoke and CO detectors, and panic buttons. Our solutions can be professionally monitored or self-monitored, and we integrate smart locks and Alexa-enabled devices so you can manage security from anywhere. With decades of experience, we focus on systems that are reliable, intuitive, and tailored to your needs. Our goal is to provide peace of mind, knowing that your property, family, or business is protected with technology that works seamlessly and consistently.
