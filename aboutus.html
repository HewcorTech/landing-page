Who We Are

Our team brings decades of hands-on experience to every project. From residential upgrades to fully integrated systems, we focus on doing the job right, with attention to detail and a commitment to quality and customer service.

Our expertise includes:

  • 35+ years in electrical services: From simple fixes to full-scale commercial and industrial projects.
  • 20+ years in low-voltage and home A/V systems: designing and installing setups that make life more connected and enjoyable.
  • 10+ years in security solutions: protecting families and businesses with reliable protection and peace of mind

Every project is guided by care, professionalism, and a focus on what matters most to our clients. When you work with Tech On Demand, you’re choosing a team that values your trust and respects your space.

OUR TEAM

Jason Hewitt

Jason is the Owner/Operator of Tech On Demand and Hewcor Technologies, bringing an extensive background in telecommunications, home audio-visual systems, and industrial networking. With years of hands-on experience and a passion for innovation, he leads the company with a focus on delivering reliable, high-quality solutions that seamlessly connect technology with everyday life.

Andrew Fehr

Andrew is the General Manager of Tech On Demand and Hewcor Technologies, with a Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering Technologies, a Master Electrician designation, and a Red Seal Journeyperson certification.
